Charizard – My Favorite Pokémon

Charizard is the best

Charizard is a Fire/Flying-type Pokémon. It's the final form of Charmander and looks like a fierce orange dragon with wings!

Best Moves

  • Flamethrower
  • Dragon Claw
  • Air Slash
  • Blast Burn

Why I Love It

Charizard is powerful, iconic, and looks super cool! It was also one of my first favorite Pokémon from the anime and games.
